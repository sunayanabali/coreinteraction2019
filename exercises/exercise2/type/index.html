A new modular typeface structured solely out of triangles. The pattern consists of triangles joining at the baseline or flat edge of each shape forming the stems within a modular structure. The inspiration for this design originated from a contrast of beliefs. A vast amount of influence from the Bauhaus movement following their idea of simplicity and that design should be used for purpose and the purpose alone, eliminating decorative factors and Wolf Wiegert, a particular piece displayed a pattern made up within the shape of a circle, which gave me the idea of creating one solid shape out of several small shapes. This typeface is a variation of triangles facing different sides creating diamonds and curves. It follows the Bauhaus belief “form follows function”.
