What happens when you create a flexbox?

The Flexbox Layout aims at providing a more efficient way to lay out, align and distribute space among items in a container, even when their size is unknown and/or dynamic. The main idea behind the flex layout is to give the container the ability to alter its items' width/height to fill the page. The automatic positioning for flex boxes is rows. Defining which arrangement you want can provide coders with variations of layouts that can be easily created.
