FUCK CONTENT

In some designers’ eyes, form is seen as irrelevant as long as function, or in this case content, is lacking in (the always subjective) value.

What Rock tries to argue is that form’s job is to manipulate, treat, and shape content to have meaning. Therefore as a designer, it is our job to create ways in which to make content meaningful,through form.

We seem to accept the fact that developing content is more essential than shaping it, that good content is the measure of good design.

Throughout this articles Rocks Main point is that the instead of a focus on “what,” designers need to emphasize the “how.”
