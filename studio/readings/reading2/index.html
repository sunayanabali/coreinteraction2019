Reading 2

“Exercises in style”

"you can really discover 99 different ways to speak 1 language" This reading reinforced the fact that one story can be told and interpreted in multiple ways, restyling one simple story into different styles. It's all about the perspective.
There are many ways of presenting information,however does it all translate accurately to its original script or does the tone, mood change?
Each revision of the story was different and altered my feeling about each. A new perspective can be an effective way to show content as well as a mood that could go along with it.
